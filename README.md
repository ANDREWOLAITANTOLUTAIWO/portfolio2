## My Education

-	Ph.D. Surveying and Geoinformatics,		University of Lagos

I specialize on using geoinformatics to solve environmental, socioeconomic and engineering problems. My research is on modelling water consumption using geospatial technology and machine learning techniques. Integrating geospatial technology, GIS and machine learning techniques, the research highlights the influence of geospatial features on performance of predictive machine learning models to solve the problem of access to clean water relative to WASH standards. 

-	M.Sc. Surveying and Geoinformatics,		University of Lagos

My education includes geospatial data acquisition, processing, analysis, management and applications. I specialize in Geographic Information System (GIS) and Remote Sensing applications to environmental, social and economic studies. My dissertation focused on the development of a spatial database for traffic management and supply chain analysis in a mega city. I developed a database using GIS to integrate spatial and non-spatial data to manage traffic incidences and support road users to make informed decisions.
